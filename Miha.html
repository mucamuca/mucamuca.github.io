Anna Ott

Miha Majcen

Miha have shown great interest into work of hub:raum while searching for suitable internship in last year of Neuropsychology masters program. Since his biggest passions are cutting-edge digital technology and entrepreneurship he realized Investment Management Internship with hub:raum professionals and analyzing Europe’s best Start Up business plans is exactly what he wants.

We decided Miha is suitable candidate because he developed excellent analytical mind by his extensive involvement in scientific research including neuroeconomics, which he chose for his master’s thesis. Besides, he is extremely emotionally intelligent and open-minded person, who genuinely enjoys human interaction and brings joy into the office.

Before joining hub:raum, Miha demonstrated determination by starting/joining numerous projects, such as microbrewery, household aquaponics system, organising private tours around his home country etc. where he developed great organisational & communication skills as well as sense for entrepreneurship.

In free time Miha is also very creative, either by making music, cooking or producing business ideas.

Since we are wise progressive team, our HR expert Anna knows HR should change and CVs are dead, Miha convinced us by answering to these short questions.

What is your availability? (when and how long)

I am available from March 1. For up to six months.

What is your type of internship(mandatory or voulentary) and why you want to intern at hub:raum?

This and next answer will be a little longer since Internship at hub:raum is exactly what I am looking for and my passion for Start Ups and digital world is just too great! (laughs) :) Technically it is mandatory internship but by determination it is voluntary - I could intern at university without effort, but I want to gain experience in entrepreneurship and achieve more than my schoolmates. I want to improve scientific analytical knowledge in a challenging business environment and meet inspiring people in Start Up Centre of Europe - Berlin. Analyzing business plans for tech start-ups is perfect job for me. In future I see myself as either running or being a big part of successful tech start up. And the hub:raum’s focus on AI, IoT and SaaS is just where my interest is! I am actually learning to code in order to write neural networks and join machine learning Start Up.

What you want to learn in your internship and (if there is) what is your future career plan?

I want to learn how financing of start-ups works, what are the challenges and what is the competition. What state digital technology currently is at, what demand is after and what products are profitable. How tech start up teams are formed, how are they organised and what is their vision. I would also like to learn business management, economics and financing and I am already independent ably learning those subjects in my free time from web courses.

I am at the beginning of my career, but in future I envision myself either running or being an important part of successful tech company. I am coming to you to get knowledge on how to successfully run a business as an observer and learn from people ahead of me. I see myself as part of fifth industrial revolution by bringing clean energy and artificial intelligence to people. It is not necessarily inventing something new. We already have technology to replace fossil fuels, besides robotics are already capable of doing most of manufacturing. I see myself as ambassador of newest technology with bringing it to the people by representing good and successful inventions. Finally I want to improve my German. I would have gained tremendous amount of invaluable experience working in Investing Management team as an intern at hub:raum.

Tell us your personal definition of what work means to you?

For me work is sharing your knowledge with the world while learning at the same time. A never ending loop of personal growth and manufacturing. Nevertheless we should be conscious that each and every step we make in career is bigger then the last one, because the more we learn the more we can give and the happiness we create by this is the fuel we need to continue.

Describe your passion for start-ups and all things digital.

I joined first start-up at 20. We were making household aquaponics growing system. We had to do anything from design to marketing, it was extremely hard but results were fulfilling more than anything! Internet now provides us information to the extent unimaginable even 10 years ago, and the kids from the street are suddenly entrepreneurs. And this is what drives me - literally everything become possible and is actually becoming real every day, because of startups.

I am great fan and admirer of Ray Kurzweil and Elon Musk, and I literally expect people being digital cyborges and colonizing mars in 10years. In 20years Ray Kurzweil predicts what he calls singularity – when machines will become as smart as people, I agree with him. And as neuroscientist, Idea that we will be able to live in a computer simulation in 20 years drives me more than any story from childhood did. And I know it will happen, everything is already set in this direction.
