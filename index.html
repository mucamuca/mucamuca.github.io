Berlin

By lots of people Berlin is called the new Silicon Valley. Not sure if this is really true, but this is definitely due to the founder friendly eco-system the German capital has to offer: next to relatively cheap living costs, Berlin has a tremendous scene of creative people that thrive innovative ideas. This attracts talent from all over the world and all areas to Berlin. Therefore, Berlin is the perfect place to look for new, disruptive ideas and this is why hub:raum choose Berlin as its location-to-be in Germany.
